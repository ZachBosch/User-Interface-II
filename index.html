I spent the first decade and half of my adulthood working in sales. I never felt like I excelled at it, never felt like I was making any headway in 'life'. I finally realized I didn't enjoy sales, because I never sold anything I really cared about. I was never passionate about any of my products. And so begins my sojourn into the world of code. I've always been mechanically minded, love to build and create. I think learning a skill that I can take with me wherever I go, and even teach to others, is an excellent way of developing a passion for my career.
